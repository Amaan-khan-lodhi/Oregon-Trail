# 🏕️ Oregon Trail
 
A survival game where you trek through a dangerous forest trying to reach the end alive!
 
## 🎮 What's the Game About?
 
You're lost in the forest. Your goal? Travel 10.0 km to escape. But it's not easy:
- Your **health, food, and money** matter
- **Storms** can hit you anytime (bring a tent!)
- **Wolves** might attack at night
- You can **hunt for food, rest, or sell what you've got**
- Find **sticks** to defend yourself
Reach 100 distance = **YOU WIN** 🎉

## 🎯 How to Play
 
**Your Stats:**
- **Health**: How alive you are (0 = dead)
- **Food**: Keep it up or you starve
- **Distance**: Progress toward escape (need 100)
- **Money**: Use it to build a tent
**What You Can Do:**
- **TRAVEL** → Move forward (costs health + food)
- **REST** → Get your health back
- **HUNT** → Find food (risky!)
- **SELL** → Sell food for money
- **TENT** → Buy protection from storms (costs 300 Rs)
- **EAT** → Restore health using food
- **INV** → Check your items
- **SAVE/LOAD** → Save your progress
- **EXIT** → Quit game
**Random Events:**
- Find sticks on the ground (fight wolves with them!)
- Storms damage you (tent protects you)
- Wolf attacks (worse at night, worse without a tent)
## 🕐 Day & Night Cycle
 
- **Day**: Safer, storms less likely, wolves easier to handle
- **Night**: Scary! More wolf attacks, more damage
## 💡 Tips to Win
 
1. Build a tent ASAP (protects from storms + wolf damage)
2. Hunt early to get food
3. Keep health above 50% always
4. Sticks are your best friend at night
5. Rest when health is low
6. Save your game often!
## 🔧 Technical Stuff
 
- **Language**: C++
- **Graphics**: raylib
- **Features**: Save/Load system, Random events, Day/Night cycle, Visual effects
## 📝 Requirements
 
- **Windows 10+**
- 512 MB RAM minimum
- 1024x768 screen minimum
## 🎮 Controls
 
Everything is done with **mouse clicks** on buttons at the bottom. No keyboard needed!
 
## ❓ Stuck?
 
- Can't afford tent? Sell food!
- Taking too much damage? Build tent + grab sticks
- Out of food? Hunt or rest and hunt
- Low health? Rest or eat food
